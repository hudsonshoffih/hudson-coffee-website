Frequently Asked Questions

How do I place an order?

Placing an order is easy! Simply browse our menu, select your favorite items, and click 'Add to Cart'. When you're ready, proceed to checkout and follow the instructions to complete your purchase.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept various payment methods including credit cards, debit cards, and PayPal. We also offer gift cards which can be used for purchases on our website.

Can I track my order?

Yes, once your order has been shipped, you will receive a tracking number via email. You can use this number to track the status of your delivery on our website.

Do you offer international shipping?

Currently, we only ship within the United States. We are working on expanding our shipping options to include international destinations in the near future.
